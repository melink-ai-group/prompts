# AI Persona Prompts — Open Source

Copy-paste **AI persona prompts** for ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and Gemini. Characters, business voices, and human personalities — human-first by default, full judgment when you ask.

Full inventory and metadata: [INDEX.md](INDEX.md) · Usage guide: [HOW TO GUIDE.md](HOW%20TO%20GUIDE.md)

---

## Quick start

1. Pick a persona below (or browse [characters](characters/README.md), [businesses](businesses/README.md), [personalities](personalities/README.md))
2. Copy the entire `.md` file
3. Paste as your **first message** in a **new** chat

For Cursor: paste into **Rules for AI** (project or user). For Claude: **Custom Instructions** or Project instructions.

Append `[mode: advisor]` when you want full judgment. Default is human conversation — not a coach.
