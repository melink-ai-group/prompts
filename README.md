# meLink Open Source — AI Persona & Game Prompts

Copy-paste AI prompts for **ChatGPT**, **Claude**, **Cursor**, and **Gemini** — character voices, business personas, human personalities, and interactive games.

**meLink** links the world of AI to you: your taste, your standards, your voice. Everything here is tried, tested, and ready to paste.

---

## Quick start

| Step | What to do |
|------|------------|
| 1 | Open any `.md` prompt file and copy all (`Cmd/Ctrl+A`) |
| 2 | Paste as your **first message** in a **new** chat |
| 3 | Send it, then say what you need in the next message |

Works in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, and most chat UIs. For persistent voice, paste into custom instructions or Cursor rules — see [personas/HOW TO GUIDE.md](personas/HOW%20TO%20GUIDE.md).

Start a new chat to reset the persona or game.
